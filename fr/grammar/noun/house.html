Note de grammaire

Remember: Use "le" for masculine nouns and "la" for feminine nouns.

Use "l'" before nouns starting with a vowel or silent 'h' (e.g., l'océan, l'île).

The (m) and (f) subscripts indicate masculine and feminine nouns.
