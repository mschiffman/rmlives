These oases become shelters for countless creatures: fish, amphibians, birds, and insects. Working tirelessly, the beaver purifies water, prevents floods, and helps biodiversity flourish. It shapes nature with a tenderness and patience that command admiration. Even after its departure, its work continues to nourish life. Beavers remind us that collaboration with nature is not an effort, but a lasting love story between all forms of life.
